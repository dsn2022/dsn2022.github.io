Registration

You can register for DSN 2022 here: https://cvent.me/2ExV4r

Please email registration+DSN@computer.org if you need any registration assistance. For questions regarding author submissions, please contact Saman Zonouz at saman.zonouz@rutgers.edu.

The conference registration fees are:

  • Full conference:
    • IEEE/IFIP member: $850
    • IEEE Student member: $595
    • IEEE Life member: $470
    • Non-member: $1020
    • Student non-member: $715
  • Workshops only:
    • IEEE/IFIP member: $400
    • IEEE Student member: $280
    • IEEE Life member: $280
    • Non-member: $480
    • Student non-member: $340

Each main conference paper must have a full (non-student) conference registration. Each workshop paper must have either a workshop or a full conference registration.

Cancellation policy:
All refund/cancellation requests must be received by June 2nd 2022. There will be an administrative fee of $100 USD ($50 USD for workshops only registrations) deducted from each refund. You can modify or cancel your registation using the "Already Registered?" link on the registration site.
